Name

Description

Haste Up – Increase attack speed by 4% per level.

Special Attack – Unlocks a special for all characters.

Growth – Increases weapon/special damage by 2% per level.

EXP Gain – Increases the amount of EXP gained by 4% per level.

Money Gain Up – Increases the amount of HoloCoins gained by 20% per level.

Reroll – Grants a use of Reroll when leveling up.

Enhancement Rate Up – Increases the chance of success during enhancements by 3% per level. .

Defense Up – Increases defense, reducing damage taken by 3% per level.
